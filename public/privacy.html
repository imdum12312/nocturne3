Privacy Policy
Nocturne — web proxy
← Home

This policy describes what Nocturne does and does not collect when you use the service.

What We Don't Collect

  • The content of proxied pages or your search queries.
  • Account information — Nocturne has no accounts or sign-ups.
  • Passwords or form data you submit through the proxy.

What's Stored Locally

Nocturne stores the following in your browser (localStorage). None of this is transmitted to the server:

  • Your chosen search engine, tab cloak, and UI preferences.
  • Editor content, filename, language, and preferences on the Code page.
  • AI chat history and API key on the Code page, if used.

All local data is cleared when you clear your browser storage for this site.

Server-Side Logs

The server may retain standard connection logs (IP address, timestamp, request path) for a limited time for abuse prevention and debugging. These logs are not the content of proxied traffic — they are the same kind of logs any web server produces.

IP Blacklisting

If abuse is detected, the offending IP address may be permanently blocked from accessing Nocturne. See the Terms of Service for the full policy.

Analytics

Nocturne uses Google Analytics on its shell pages (home, apps, code, legal) to understand basic usage — page views, country-level geography, and browser/device type. It does not track what you browse through the proxy. You can opt out with any standard tracker-blocker (uBlock Origin, Privacy Badger) or Firefox's Enhanced Tracking Protection.

Third Parties

Nocturne loads fonts from Google Fonts, the Monaco editor from jsDelivr CDN, and the analytics tag from Google Tag Manager. Those providers may see that your browser requested those assets.
